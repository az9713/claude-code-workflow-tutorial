Most people use Claude Code like a chatbot. Dynamic Workflows let one Claude run a whole team of agents in a single window, and these are the three I actually use.

I walk through three dynamic workflows you can build today, in plain English, no jargon:

1. The Personalized Upgrade Guide. Point a workflow at your own Claude Code session logs and it builds an Opus 4.7 to 4.8 upgrade guide tailored to how you actually work, with copy-ready prompt upgrades.

2. The Fact-Checked Research Report. It pulls the real X threads through Apify, not just blog posts, fact-checks every claim, and tells you exactly what survived and what got cut.

3. The Setup Audit. It goes through your global and project Claude folders and finds overlapping skills, contradicting rules, and dead weight, then hands you a grouped fix list.

The thread running through all three is the one step most people skip: a second pass that verifies every finding before it reaches you. That is what turns a fast answer into one you can actually trust.

All three prompts are free at the link above, along with the 6-part framework so you can point a verified workflow at any pile of your own work.
